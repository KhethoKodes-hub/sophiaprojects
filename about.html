About Sophia Projects

Where excellence meets innovation

Sophia Projects (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic black empowered company that comprises of professionals who are adamant in securing a high level of services and quality products in a most cost effective manner.

Our vision is to provide the best customer-focused services, development and acquisition to become the most preferred brand in the South African market.

Since our humble inception, the company has grown from strength to strength. From the very beginning, the company has focused on its core principles of customer service, quality, value for money and innovation, and it is these that have enabled the company to gain the trust of customers.

The company continuously introduces new and high tech designs to remain at the fore-front of the industry.

Our Leadership

Dedicated & Experienced Team

Sophia Projects is fully managed by 3 directors on a full-time basis who are responsible for managing the day-to-day activities of the business. Their duties include:

  • Meeting with clients and understanding their needs
  • Controlling finances of the business
  • Strategic planning and business development
  • Organizing business goals and objectives
  • Hiring and managing employees
  • Motivating workers and maintaining high morale
  • Marketing and business promotion
  • Monitoring workplace and workers' performance

As a 100% Black empowered business, we are proud to contribute to economic transformation while delivering outstanding services to our clients.
